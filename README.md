# Rapido Ride Booking Analytics Dashboard

## Project Overview

This project analyzes ride-booking data to uncover demand patterns, revenue trends, vehicle performance, and operational insights. The dataset was cleaned and analyzed using SQL, while Power BI was used to create an interactive multi-page dashboard for business decision-making.

The dashboard helps answer key business questions related to customer demand, revenue generation, ride completion, and vehicle performance.

---

## Business Objective

The objective of this project is to help a ride-booking company understand:

* When customers book rides most frequently
* Which vehicle categories generate the highest revenue
* How ride demand changes across days and hours
* Which locations experience the highest activity
* How operational performance impacts revenue

## Dashboard Structure

### 1. Overview Dashboard

Provides a high-level summary of business performance.

Key Metrics:

* Total Bookings
* Total Revenue
* Average Fare
* Ride Completion Metrics
* Revenue by Vehicle Type
* Most Frequent Pickup Locations
* Most Frequent Drop Locations

Purpose:

Provides stakeholders with a quick snapshot of overall platform performance.

---

### 2. Time & Demand Analysis

Analyzes customer demand patterns throughout the day and week.

Key Metrics:

* Peak Pickup Hour
* Peak Booking Day
* Average Rides per Hour
* Maximum Ride Volume
* Weekday vs Weekend Analysis

Visualizations:

* Hourly Demand Trends
* Booking Distribution by Day
* Demand Heatmap (Day vs Hour)

Purpose:

Helps identify peak demand periods and optimize driver allocation.

---

### 3. Revenue & Vehicle Analysis

Evaluates revenue generation and vehicle performance.

Key Metrics:

* Total Revenue
* Revenue Contribution %
* Revenue per Kilometer
* Distance Covered
* Vehicle-wise Performance

Visualizations:

* Revenue by Vehicle Type
* Distance Analysis
* Vehicle Performance Comparison
* Revenue Efficiency Metrics
* Scatter Plot Analysis

Purpose:

Identifies the most profitable vehicle categories and operational opportunities.

## Key Insights

* Demand varies significantly across different hours of the day.
* Certain locations contribute a large share of total bookings.
* Vehicle categories show varying revenue contributions and operating efficiency.
* Peak demand periods present opportunities for optimized driver allocation.
* Revenue performance differs across vehicle segments and ride volumes.

---

## Business Recommendations

* Increase driver availability during peak demand windows.
* Prioritize high-performing vehicle categories during surge periods.
* Monitor cancellation rates in high-demand zones.
* Optimize resource allocation based on hourly booking trends.
* Focus operational planning around recurring demand patterns.
